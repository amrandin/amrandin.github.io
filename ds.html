Data Science is the scientific study of data to understand and analyze insights from structured and unstructured data. It is a multi-disciplinary field of study that employs the scientific method and employs techniques and theories from the field of mathematics, statistics, computer science, and information science. Data science aims to unify statistics, data analysis, machine learning and related methods to further understand and analyze actual phenomena data may generate.
